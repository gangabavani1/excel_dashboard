# excel_dashboard
**Report 1: Sales & Product Performance Dashboard**
Goal:Analyze what is selling, which categories are most profitable, and how discounts affect our volume.
5 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Total Gross Revenue:Sum of (Quantity × Unit Price) before discounts.
Total Units Sold:Total sum of the Quantity column.
Average Discount Rate:The average percentage of discount given across all items.
Net Revenue:Total Revenue minus Total Discounts.
Most Popular Category: The category with the highest number of orders.
**5 Visual Charts**
Sales by Product: A Bar Chart showing the total Net Revenue for each product (e.g., Laptop vs. SSD).
Category Distribution: A Pie/Doughnut Chart showing the split between "Electronics" and "Accessories."
Revenue vs. Discount: A Combo Chart (Line and Cluster Column) showing Revenue and Discount amounts per product.
Quantity by Month: A Line Chart showing the trend of units sold based on the OrderDateTime.
Top 5 Order Items: A Bar Chart showing which Item IDs generated the most quantity.
**3 Interactive Slicers**
Category
ProductName
Month (Derived from OrderDateTime)

**Report 2:Customer & Regional Insights Dashboard**
Goal: Understand who our customers are, where they live, and how they prefer to shop.

**5 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**
Total Customer Count: Total number of unique Customer IDs.
Average Order Value (AOV): Total Amount divided by Total Orders.
Top Performing Region: The region with the highest total sales amount.
Order Success Rate: Percentage of orders marked as "Delivered."
Gender Split: Ratio of Male to Female customers.

**5 Visual Charts**
Region-wise Revenue: A Map or Column Chart showing Total Amount by Region (North, South, East, West).
Customer Gender Analysis: A Pie Chart showing the percentage of orders placed by Male vs. Female.
Order Status Breakdown: A Funnel or Column Chart showing the count of "Delivered," "Shipped," "Processing," and "Cancelled."
Channel Preference: A Bar Chart comparing sales coming from the "App" vs. "Website."
City-wise Top Sales: A Bar Chart showing the top 5 cities by Total Amount.

**3 Interactive Slicers**
Region
Status (Delivered, Shipped, etc.)
PreferredChannel
